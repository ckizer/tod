Rules of the Game:
The current player's name will appear in the box at the bottom of the game screen.
Each player may choose either a truth or a dare on their turn.
The player may choose either to complete the truth or dare prompt or to wimp out.
If the player completes the prompt, the difficulty of the prompt will be added to the player's score.
If the player wimps out, no points will be added to the player's score.
The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!
