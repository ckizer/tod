Rules of the Game:
The current player's name will appear in the box at the bottom of the game screen.
Each player will receive a dare on their turn.
The player may chose to complete the dare or to wimp out.
If the player completes the dare, the difficulty of the dare will be added to the player's score.
If the player wimps out, no points will be added to the player's score.
The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!
